Cryptography

      The cryptography is associated with process of converting ordinary text into unreadable text and vice versa.

Steganography

      The steganography is  the concept of hiding or retrieving messages within the image.

Combination of cryptography and steganography

      Here the messages were encrypted before hiding withing the image and the messages were decrypted after retrieved from the image.
